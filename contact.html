FAQs

Is the analyzer free?

Yes — completely free. We support the site through Google AdSense advertising.

How accurate is it?

Our AI looks up real published specs and applies established audio engineering principles. Use results as a strong guide, not a substitute for professional advice.

Are my components saved?

No. The site doesn't store data between sessions. You'll need to re-enter your system each visit.

My component wasn't recognized?

Try using the full manufacturer name and exact model number. Older or regional products may have limited published data.

Can I suggest a feature?

Absolutely — use the form and select "Feature Suggestion." We'd love to hear your ideas.
