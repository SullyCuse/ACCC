Frequently Asked Questions

Is the analyzer free to use?

Yes, AudioChainHiFi.com is completely free. We support the site through Google AdSense advertising.

How accurate is the analysis?

Our AI looks up published manufacturer specifications and applies established audio engineering principles. Results are a strong starting point but are not a substitute for professional advice. Spec sheets can vary between production runs.

Are my components saved between visits?

No. The site does not store your component data between sessions. You'll need to re-enter your system each visit.

My component wasn't found — what should I do?

Try entering the full manufacturer name and exact model number. Some older or regional products may have limited published spec data. You can also describe the specs manually if you have them.

Can I suggest a new feature?

Absolutely — use the contact form and select "Feature Suggestion." We read every message.

Response Time

We typically respond to messages within 2–5 business days. For urgent privacy requests, please indicate this in your message.
