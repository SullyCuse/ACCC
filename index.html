Why It Matters

Stop guessing.
Start knowing.

Building a hi-fi system requires matching dozens of technical specifications. A single mismatch — the wrong loading impedance for your cartridge, a gain structure that clips — can rob you of the sound quality you paid for.

  • Impedance mismatches cause distortion and speaker damage
  • Incorrect phono loading deadens cartridge high-frequency response
  • Poor gain staging increases noise floor and reduces dynamics
  • Wrong connection types (RCA vs XLR) degrade signal integrity
