1. Introduction

Welcome to AudioChainHiFi.com ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect and use information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices when you visit and use our website.

By using our site, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide: AudioChainHiFi.com does not require account creation or personal information to use our tool. Audio component data you enter is sent to our AI service only to generate your analysis — we do not store it.

Automatically collected: When you visit, our hosting provider and third-party services may collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Date and time of visit

Preferences: Your cookie consent choice is stored in your browser's localStorage and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Information

We use automatically collected information to operate and improve our site, understand how visitors use our service, detect security issues, and display relevant advertising (with your consent). Component data you enter is used solely to generate your compatibility analysis.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We and our partners use cookies for:

  • Advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history.
  • Analytics: To understand site traffic in aggregate.
  • Preferences: Your cookie consent choice is stored locally in your browser.

When you first visit, we display a consent banner. You may accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change this at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for our site, or through your browser's cookie settings.

5. Google AdSense and Advertising

We participate in Google AdSense, operated by Google LLC. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve advertisements relevant to your interests.

How Google uses your data: Google may use information about your visits to this and other websites to show you personalized ads. Google's advertising cookies enable it and its partners to serve ads based on your interests across the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings
  • Visit www.aboutads.info/choices

For more information, visit How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Processes component data for analysis. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify: Hosts our website and forms. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense: Serves advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Serves web fonts used on our site.

7. Children's Privacy

AudioChainHiFi.com is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access to, deletion of, or opt-out from processing of your personal data. Because we collect minimal personal information and require no account creation, most requests can be addressed by clearing your browser data and opting out of advertising. For other requests, please use our Contact page.

9. Data Security

Our site is served over HTTPS. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect information we handle. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the most recent update at the top of this page. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
