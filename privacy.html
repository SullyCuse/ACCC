1. Introduction

Welcome to AudioChainHiFi.com ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect and use information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices when you visit and use our website at AudioChainHiFi.com.

By using our site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our site.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide: AudioChainHiFi.com does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our compatibility checker tool. The audio component information you enter into the tool (component names, connection types) is transmitted to our AI analysis service to generate your results.

Automatically collected information: When you visit our website, certain information is automatically collected by our server and third-party services, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (the site that linked you to us)
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

Cookie consent preference: We store your cookie consent decision locally in your browser's localStorage. This is a functional storage that is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Information

We use automatically collected information to:

  • Operate and improve our website
  • Understand how visitors use our service
  • Detect and prevent security issues
  • Display relevant advertisements (with your consent)

Audio component data you enter is used solely to generate your compatibility analysis via our AI service. We do not store, sell, or share your component data.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. We and our third-party partners use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements based on your browsing history and interests.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand site traffic and visitor behavior in aggregate.
  • Functional storage: We store your cookie consent preference in your browser's localStorage (not a cookie, but similar in function).

When you first visit our site, we display a consent banner. You may accept or decline non-essential cookies. If you decline, we will not load advertising cookies, though the site will remain fully functional. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for our site.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling all cookies may affect the functionality of some websites you visit.

5. Google AdSense and Advertising

We participate in Google AdSense, a third-party advertising program operated by Google LLC. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to serve advertisements on our site that are relevant to your interests.

How Google uses your data for advertising: Google may use information about your visits to this and other websites to show you personalized advertisements. Google's advertising cookies enable it and its partners to serve ads based on your interests and your visit to our site and other sites across the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising from Google by:

  • Visiting Google Ads Settings
  • Visiting www.aboutads.info/choices (NAI opt-out)
  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

Opting out of personalized ads does not remove all advertisements — you will still see ads, but they will be less relevant to your interests.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, please visit How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Processes your component data to generate compatibility analyses. Component data is sent to Anthropic's API and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify: Hosts our website and processes form submissions. Subject to Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense: Serves advertisements. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Serves web fonts used on our site. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

AudioChainHiFi.com is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete that information promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request information about personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to opt out of advertising: Opt out of personalized advertising as described in Section 5.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (for EU/EEA residents).

Because we collect minimal personal information and do not require account creation, most requests can be addressed by clearing your browser data and opting out of advertising as described above. For other requests, please contact us via our Contact page.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we handle. Our site is served over HTTPS (encrypted connection). However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any information transmitted to or from our site.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will indicate the date of the most recent update at the top of this page. Your continued use of our site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to provide notice on our website prior to the change becoming effective.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our Contact page.

We will respond to inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
